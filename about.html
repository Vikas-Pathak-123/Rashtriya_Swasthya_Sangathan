About Us

Welcome to Rashtriya Swasthya Sangathan

aims and objectives of the Society.

  1. To open/maintain/assist/establish and run Sate of Art Blood banks in different states across PAN India.

  2. To open, found, establish, promote, set up, run, maintain, assist, finance, support and/or aid or help in the setting up and/or maintaining and/or running hospitals charitable dispensaries, physiotherapy centres, mobile dispensaries, maternity homes, child welfare centres, Old age homes and other similar institutions or centres for rendering or providing medical relief.

  3. To make arrangements for mobile emergency Van/ambulance for the victims of road accidents, to provide help to pregnant ladies to shift them to nearby hospital, also to provide assistance to the old people for the emergency.

  4. To provide nursing aid to the needy people especially for old age men and women, orphan, ill etc.

  5. To open centres/institutes for diagnostic curative and research of Cancer, ADS, T.B. and other deadly diseases.

  6. To establish, run, maintain, manage and control the various Medical/colleges, Training institution / courses like, Medical, Dental, Para Medical, Ayurvedic, Unani, Homeopathic, Naturopathy, Pharmacy, Nursing, Laboratory, Occupational Therapy, Dietician, Beautician, Acupuncture and Acupressure Therapy (after getting proper permission from the competent authority of it is required).

  7. Imparting Educating Technical Management and medical services especially for the research training, development eradication and treatment of epidemics and dangerous/ fatal diseases like Aids, Cancer , tuberculosis, leprosy epilepsy, chicken pox. Diabetes Jaundice hepatitis Malaria, Harla Dengue Fever encephalitis, Meningitis Goiter Blindness Deafness Dumbness and heart diseases.

  8. Imparting education and information to society / masses for implementation of family planning/welfare schemes by way of arranging/ conducting conferences, seminars road shows films, Puppet shows , Distribution of literature/ pamphlets brochures and hoarding etc.

  9. Imparting education and information to/society/masses for eradicating & control of AIDS and other final diseases & implementation of family planning/welfare schemes by way of arranging conducting conferences seminars, road shows. Films, Puppet shows , Distribution of literature/pamphlets/ brochures and hoarding etc. especially in rural areas.

  10. To work for operating the Rural Mobile Clinic Schemes {RMCs) for providing primary Medical and Health Care & Medicines to the needy poor at their door step in rural and remote area.

  11. Imparting education, Technical Education and Medical Education and knowledge.

  12. Imparting education, research, training, development and a strengthening of the ancient Indian system of medicine “Ayurveda” and encourage the use of herbal Ayurveda drugs/medicines and cosmetics by the society/masses and also promoting cure of diseases by naturo pathy and Yoga.

  13. To work for the Establishment of Rural Health Centres in villages, especially in those rural areas where such health care facilities are non existent, to augment the efforts of state towards providing widespread medical and health services to the society/masses.

  14. To work for the fast, safe & proper disposal of Hospital waste for prevention its reuse/recycling towards prevention and control of deadly/fatal diseases like AIDS etc.

  15. Social supplement of mentally retired children especially to uplift/improve their social moral and education status.

  16. To work against child abuse, rape trafficking in girl child female infanticide and superstition relating to women.

  17. To help and assist the various Govt. agencies in augmenting their efforts toward the implementation of various schemes of social welfare, family planning/welfare and population control etc.
