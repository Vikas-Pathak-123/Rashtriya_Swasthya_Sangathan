While certain sections of society work towards a future in which they can thrive, vulnerable families and individuals around India still have basic, immediate needs. Rashtriya Swasthya Sangathan as an organization are willing to support the ones suffering. To create awareness about Preventive Health checkup, we organize free Health checkup and awareness camp with the support of government health organisation in rural and remote areas across India to make sure impoverished families get their health treatment at the right time besides we are all set to serve the older people and senior citizens who live their lives away from the privileges. In addition to that we focus to work towards making necessities like availability of blood, treatment of illness and diseases, available to the deprived discontinuously throughout India. Nursing the underprivileged, organising aids awareness programs that aims to empower and increase the awareness to participants of HIV/AIDS, its impact, management and availability of support systems and blood donation camps to keep up with the challenges of thalassemia treatment is our prime objective.




Blood Donation

We support, mantain and run blood banks and blood donation camps in different states across PAN India. As a blood bank organisation we provide free blood to needy people. We often participate in the collection process as well as the procedures that foll...ow it. We organise some of the events to aware people about blood donation myths and motivate them to DONATE BLOOD AND SAVE LIFE! Medical examination of blood donors are always carried out by a doctors. We provide facilities 24*7.

Mobile Clinic

In rural India, we use mobile medical clinics which are useful models for delivering health promotion, education, and care. We use fewer providers for larger catchment areas compared to traditional clinic models in resource limited settings, which is especially ...useful in areas with shortages of healthcare providers and a wide geographical distribution of patients. We bring such needed health services directly to underserved populations. We benefits the communities by making health care more affordable and accessible, which in turn improves patient outcomes. Old Age Homes

Craving to establish ourselves as an NGO for Old Age Homes/senior citizens in India, Hindrise Social Welfare Foundation is endeavouring to uplift the socio-economic status of abandoned and neglected senior citizens residing in various parts of ... India. We are moving ahead with the belief that our efforts would help them live a noble and respectable life. We have done the ground-level research, and we have identified the requirements of older adults suffering due to the complications concerning changes in the environment and life-related challenges. A roof over their heads is a critical need of the elder who are destitute, sick and abandoned by family and those uprooted by disasters. Rashtriya Swasthya Sangathan supports the model homes for the senior citizen and it also raise the funds for the old age homes. Those funds used by the old age homes to provide basic necessities and used for the welfare of senior people, because for us EVERYONE DESERVES LOVE.
